Gameplay:

1. How to click the right choice:


1a. If you see the racket/red move down then press "back" button

1b. If you see the racket move up/re√∏d move up when contacting the ball then press "top" button

1c. If you see the racket not a lot up or down while contacting the ball then press "dead" button



2. If you make the correct choice just press anywhere on the bottom half of the screen to continue or click load video to see why your choice was wrong.

3. If you make the wrong choice then there will be two buttons on the top half and nothing on the bottom such that it is hard to skip watching the video which automatically plays to show you why you chose the wrong answer.

4. To the right of the video player will be a try again button to restart you to level 1 again.

5. There is a timer to the top right which turns more red as its invisible hand turns clockwise. When one 1/4 pie shape of red appears then that means 1/4 of the total time has elapsed. Inside is a long

6. If you have played one game and failed you see a crown icon above the score board in every new reattempt in order to show what to beat next.

7. The score:[insert number] will be your current score during your attempt.

8. The black heart with the white number will count down by one each time you lose a life.

9. You will be able to access the leaderboards while in the continue pop up, retry pop or in the main menu. In the continue pop up and retry pop up it will be all the day on the top middle of the screen and the leaderboard icon will be on the bottom of the start button in the main menu section.
